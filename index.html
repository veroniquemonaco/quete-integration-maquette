bold and em style

BY MICHEAL ON OCTOBER 26,2006

If tou need to make more room for the blog titles and keep them on one line, simply adjust the size of the #content H1 style. Bold text looks like this. It's 'Arial Black' and #FFF just like the title and the headers, however, if the person viewing the site removed 'Arial Black' then the font-weight:600 property will take care of bolding it as much as possible.

Emphesized or italisized text looks like this.I don't think the look of Arial's italics stands out, so i usually swap it out with Georgia.

HOVER NEWS & QUOTES

BY MICHEAL ON OCTOBER 18,2006

Right under the navigation menu to the right you will see a heading called Recent Updates. If you hover the new items a short summery will pop up and allow you to click to get another page. You can use this as a link list giving people a short description of what your link is about. I learned the technique from Stu at CSS Play. The original demo was to display a small photo gallery this way but i adapted it for text.
